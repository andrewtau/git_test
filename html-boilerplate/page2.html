CSS Box Model

What is it?

It contains 3 different components: The Margin, Border, and Padding.

Margin: Pushes things away (from the item or 'box'). Creates empty space WITHOUT the background color.

Border: Creates a border around the 'box' to manipulate. Has 3 other properies associated with it;
border-width, border-color, and border-style.

Padding: Creates empty space WITH the background color.
